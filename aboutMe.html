A little about me

I like to share my opinion. It’s rewarding when an idea helps encourage someone to think differently or feel more confident in decisions. Ideas aren’t always right but hey it’s the thought that counts.


I’ve always been interested in hardware. As a kid it was building with legos and Stephen Biesty’s Incredible Cross-Sections picture book. I enjoyed either direction. Building piece by piece or seeing the breakdown of something like the space shuttle chapter.

I received an early lesson in design tradeoffs. Nintendo's Game Boy went from Amtrak train rides with AA batteries and a worm-like light attached to hurricane season with a rechargeable battery and no headphone jack...

I was late online. AOL, Xbox Live, MySpace, I was grounded in backyard sports.


I have been thinking more about software.
